Design

Our multidisciplinary design team offer supports for all our channel partners. Vision collaborates, develops and communicates a brands culture, values and principles across the physical space.

Production

We produce everything in-house or, for international projects, by collaborating with our business partners. This benefits our clients as we have complete control over quality and deadlines. It also allows us to innovate using unique solutions with wood, glass, a.v. and lighting.

Installation

Our installers have years of experience in overcoming challenges of any kind to deliver the project on time. The installation team works closely with our designers, meticulously implementing the initial project to meet and surpass our client’s expectations.

project Management

We assign a project manager to liaise between our internal teams and the building contractor, the architects and any agent involved in every project. This allows our clients to keep track of the progress and maintain a direct link with each one of our teams.
